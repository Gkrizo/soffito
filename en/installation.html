Installation of stretch ceilings

The average installation time is several hours. Without additional payment, we install stretch ceilings on weekends and most holidays.

We have experienced experts, so the process of installing a stretch ceiling is quick and clean.

Taking measurements

Order a master for free measurement using the form or by phone +357 25 580 418.

The master technologist performs the measurement of the room and makes a drawing. The exact cost of installing a stretch ceiling is reported immediately after the measurement and includes materials and works. After the contract is signed and the installation date is agreed.

Preworks

Preparations for installation of a stretched ceiling are minimal. To perform the work, it is required to provide access to the walls where possible and remove the indoor plants.

Installation

Stretch ceiling is installed on the level of a horizontal line, applied with a laser device. PVC or aluminum baguette (profile) is being installed around the perimeter of the room.
If there is a chandelier, the fixture is installed on the main ceiling and electrical wiring is laid.

The stretching of the canvas is the most important moment in the installation. First, it is unfolded on the special holders in the room. Right after that the air is heated to 50 degrees by a heat gun in order for the material to become elastic. Further the canvas is stretched with the help of mounting blades and the edges of the canvas are being installed to the profile.

The final stage of installation is the attachment of the reinforcing ring for a chandelier or lamp and the installation of a special decorative tape in order to hide the joints of the wall and ceiling canvas.

Subject to all requirements, the guarantee on a tension ceiling makes 10 years.
