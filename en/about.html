Textures of stretch ceilings:

  • Glossy

    Thanks to the mirror surface, they are suitable for creating a fashionable design of rooms, achieving an unusual and aesthetic appearance. The reflective power of such ceilings visually increases the space, which is especially important in confined spaces, and also enhances the effectiveness of lighting devices.

  • Matte

    Matte texture is visually close to the surface of the classic bleached ceiling, because it does not reflect glare. Ideally smooth, inaccessible to other solutions, the surface, gives the room a restrained elegance. Matt textures are used in large rooms, when there is no task of visually increasing the space.

  • Satin

    Satin stretch ceilings have an original texture that gives them an elusive mother-of-pearl ebb. Due to the specific microrelief of the canvas, minor reflection effect makes it possible to create a soft play of light. The impeccably smooth surface looks very attractive both under natural and artificial light.

  • Textile

    They are made of polyester fabric of complex weaving with polyurethane impregnation, the strength of the canvas is 15 times higher than that of PVC film. The surface is matte and slightly rough. Textile ceilings are perfectly combined with interiors in a classical style. They also can be used for photo printing and paint, do not afraid of cuts and wash well.

Design ideas for stretch ceilings:

  • Multi-level

    Multi-level stretch ceilings help to realize the most interesting design projects. They allow you to design ceilings of a very complex configuration, with differences in height and a variety of lighting elements. Also, multi-level stretch ceilings are great for zoning of premises.

  • Art printing

    The image of your choice is applied to the ceiling by an industrial plotter. The available width is 3 meters. UV ink used for printing conforms to environmental safety standards. They are moisture resistant and do not burn out in the sun, so the clarity of the pattern and the saturation of the colors persist for a long time.

  • Curvilinear seam

    Achieved by the method of curvilinear soldering of canvases. The combination of different colors and textures in one plane allows you to realize interesting design ideas. Similarly to multi-level solutions, the curvilinear seam helps visually mark out a part of the space and zone the premises, favorably differing by a minimal decrease in the height of the ceiling.

  • 3D-design

    The flexibility of PVC canvas allows you to go beyond the two dimensions of a conventional ceiling and create a design in three-dimensional space. The creativity is limited to the imagination of the designer and the needs of the interior, rather than the capabilities of the material. Stretched ceilings are an ideal tool for creating an expressive and striking interior.

  • Lighting solutions

    Glossy, translucent and transparent canvases offer a number of interesting and original lighting solutions: the room can be illuminated by a backlighting around the perimeter of the room, light crystals or even the whole surface of the ceiling with the help of light panels. The most popular options are available in the section Lighting.

  • Perforated ceilings

    Represent a two-level construction from the main layer, usually colored, and underlying canvass with holes of arbitrary shapes and dimensions. Due to the presence of two levels, perforated ceilings provide an opportunity to create interesting visual effects, changing the volume and depth of the room.
