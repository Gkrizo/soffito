Lighting solutions:

  • Spot

    Spot lights are directed light sources. This lighting fixture can be embedded into the surface: suspended and modular ceilings, walls, stairs. LED spotlights have low power consumption, are bright, practical and durable.

  • Lighting panels

    In the off state this is a normal stretch ceiling, but click the switch and the whole surface becomes a light source! Due to the use of a translucent film and a properly located sources, the light turns is soft, without shadows and spots. The intensity of illumination can be adjusted with the help of the control panel from very bright daylight to mild night.

  • Crystal spots

    The visible part of these lamps is decorated with a crystal crown. A special technique of glass processing allows to achieve interesting visual effects, amplifying or muffling the light, changing its color and direction. Using crystal spots with glossy textures can enhance the effect due to reflection in the mirror surface of the canvas.

  • Profile lighting

    LED strips lighting makes it possible to embody complex design projects for architectural, interior lighting and lighting design of geometric shapes and designs. LED strip is used for the original lighting of ceilings. It not only illuminates the room, but also creates interesting lighting effects.
