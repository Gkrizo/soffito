About us

"Soffito" is the first professional manufacturer and installer of stretch ceilings in Cyprus.

High quality materials, reasonable prices and professional team ensure the success of the company and the steady growth of the number of satisfied customers.

A great experience in the field of stretch ceilings allows Soffito specialists to embody complex and interesting design projects. The main range of our works is the complex provision of services for the production and turnkey installation of stretch ceilings in an apartment, house, office, shop and other premises.

Soffito only uses materials from proven manufacturers. High quality of materials and components is an integral condition of the company's corporate policy, allowing our work to please the client for many years. Soffito takes care that the company always has a wide choice of films of different textures and colors, and guarantees an impeccable appearance of stretch ceilings, their compliance with environmental standards and excellent performance characteristics.

At your service is a huge catalog of textures and colors of stretch ceilings: for office, apartment or house. It is as well possible to print any photo or picture on the canvas of the ceiling. Our professional consultants will tell you about current trends in design, help you choose the right texture and color of the film, and develop a suitable ceiling solution for your interior.

The pleasant bonuses of our company are:

  • Free measurements of your premises and price calculation;
  • The price of the ceiling is turnkey - it includes delivery and installation;
  • We install on weekdays, weekends and most holidays without extra fee;
  • The opportunity to arrange a contract at home;
  • Guarantee of the quality of installation.
