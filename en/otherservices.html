Repair of stretch ceilings

Service includes works that are not related to warranty repair.

One of the popular services is the repair of the stretch ceiling after the apartment is flooded (leaks from above). Among other services - drying and washing the stretch ceiling, installation of built-in lamps or other equipment, replacement of the cloth for a new, removal of the stretch ceiling and more.

Repair of the ceiling may be required in case of its mechanical damage, for example, cut or tear. Noticing the cut of the canvas, you need to connect its edges with adhesive tape to prevent further rupture before the arrival of specialists. People who have experienced experience should repair the stretch ceiling.

The prices for repairing the stretch ceiling and the method depend on the nature of the damage. After eliminating a small damage, the consequences of repair are almost invisible. If the cut is more than 2 cm, then there may be a slight defect on the canvas. If desired, it can be removed using decorative lining.

Draining of water from the stretch ceiling

In the event of water leakage from above, you must turn off all ceiling lights.

After you have eliminated the cause of the leak, determine approximately how much water is in the stretch ceiling. Is the ceiling hanging? In the case of a large amount of water, we recommend specialists to be called up urgently.

Attention! We do not recommend draining the water yourself! Attempts to self-dismantle the ceiling can lead to damage to the canvas, flooding the apartment and/or short circuit.

The cost of the water draining service depends on the work performed on the site. Minimal complex of works: team arrival + drainage of water + warming up the canvas with gas equipment. Additional works might include: partial dismantling or installation of a stretch ceiling, cleaning the canvas, drying the main ceiling with gas equipment, treatment with an antifungal agent of the main ceiling.

In most cases, to drain the water, the removal of entire stretch ceiling is not required and the water is drained through the technical opening for a chandelier or lamp. Everything depends on the specific case. Accurate information on how much it will cost and how long it will take to drain the water from the stretch ceiling can be given after the assessment of a specialist.
